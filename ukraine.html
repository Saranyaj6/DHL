UKRAINE SITUATION UPDATE

DHL has resumed limited shipping services in select locations of Ukraine. As we operate under elevated risk, additional charges may apply.

Service update Russia and Belarus

Our inbound services to Russia and Belarus have been suspended, which is why we are also not accepting shipments to those countries. As part of this suspension, DHL Express has also decided to stop its domestic services within Russia, effective September 1st, 2022.

We are closely monitoring the situation and are working with our customers to mitigate the impact on our international and domestic customers. We will keep you updated about any further developments.
